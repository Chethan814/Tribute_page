Bhagat Singh

"Inquilab Zindabad!"


This phrase translates to "Long Live the Revolution!" in English. It became a rallying cry for the Indian independence movement, representing the spirit of resistance and the call for freedom from colonial oppression. It encapsulates Bhagat Singh's unwavering dedication to the cause of independence and his belief in the power of revolution to bring about change.
